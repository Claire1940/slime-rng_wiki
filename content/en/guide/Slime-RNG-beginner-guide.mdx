## Upgrading Your Rolling Efficiency

In Slime RNG, the speed at which you can generate new slimes determines how quickly you can find rare variants. You should prioritize upgrading your **Roll Speed** above almost everything else in the beginning. The faster you roll, the more chances you have to trigger the game's RNG system.

Following roll speed, your next priority should be unlocking the **Auto Roll** feature. Manual clicking is sustainable for a few minutes, but Slime RNG is designed to be played over long periods, often involving passive farming. Auto Roll allows you to step away from your computer or mobile device while your character continues to collect slimes.

| Upgrade Type | Priority | Primary Benefit |

## Building a Powerful Slime Squad

Rolling the slimes is only half the battle. To earn the coins necessary for high-level upgrades, you must send your team into combat. Each slime has unique stats, and as you progress, you will need to expand your **Slime Slots**. Starting with a small team limits your damage output and coin generation.

When choosing which slimes to equip, look beyond just the rarity color. Some lower-rarity slimes may have high attack speeds or specific elemental advantages that make them more effective in certain worlds. However, generally, the higher the rarity, the better the base stats.

| Slime Rarity | Average Strength | Coin Generation | Recommended Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Common** | 1-10 | Low | Early game fodder / Fusing |
| **Rare** | 25-50 | Moderate | Starting battle squad |
| **Epic** | 100-250 | High | Mid-game progression |

## World Progression and Coin Management

As you grow stronger, you will eventually outpace the rewards provided by the starting area. Unlocking new worlds is essential for accessing better coin multipliers and higher-tier slime pools. Each world usually requires a specific amount of coins or a certain number of total rolls to enter.

To keep up with the rising costs of world entry, you must invest in **Coin Multipliers**. While roll speed helps you get slimes, coin multipliers ensure that every battle you win provides enough currency to keep your upgrades moving forward. In the 2026 version of the game, world-hopping is the fastest way to reach the endgame content.
