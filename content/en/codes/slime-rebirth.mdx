## How to Rebirth in Slime RNG: Step-by-Step

The process of performing a **slime rebirth** is straightforward, but it requires careful timing. If you rebirth too early, you might struggle to regain your momentum. If you wait too long, you are wasting time that could be spent with a higher luck multiplier.

Follow these steps to successfully rebirth:

1.  **Farm Gold:** Engage with enemies in the starting zone. Focus on high-frequency kills to stack coins quickly.
2.  **Check Your Luck:** Navigate to the upgrade menu and ensure your current luck stat meets the requirement (e.g., 1.5x for the first tier).
3.  **Unequip Items:** Some players find it beneficial to unequip certain slimes to see their raw stats before committing to the reset.
4.  **Open the Rebirth Menu:** Locate the "Rebirth" or "Repair" icon on your HUD.
5.  **Confirm the Reset:** A prompt will appear warning you about the loss of coins. Confirm to receive your permanent luck boost.

This means that even with no slimes equipped, a player with 50 rebirths will have a significantly higher chance of rolling a rare slime than a new player.

## Advanced Rebirth Tactics

Once you have mastered the basics of the **slime rebirth**, you can start using advanced tactics to shave minutes off your reset time. One popular method is "Pre-Stacking." This involves reaching the luck requirement for your *next* rebirth before you have even finished the current one. By doing this, you can trigger two or three rebirths in rapid succession once you have the gold.

Another tactic involves the "Unequip Glitch" (if still active in your version). By unequipping slimes right as a mob dies, some players have found they can trick the game into calculating rewards based on their highest-luck slime even if it wasn't used for the kill. However, developers frequently patch these bugs, so rely on standard luck-stacking for the most consistent results.

## FAQ

**Q: Does a slime rebirth reset my equipped slimes?**

A: No, your collected slimes remain in your inventory. However, your gold and certain temporary power-ups will be reset to zero. This allows you to use your best slimes to quickly regain your power in the next run.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get 2,500 coins for the first rebirth?**

A: The fastest way is to equip a slime with high damage and find a mob spawn point that you can clear in one or two hits. Avoid fighting bosses until you have at least 1.0x Luck and decent combat stats.

**Q: How much Luck do I need for a slime rebirth?**

A: For the very first rebirth, you typically need 1.5x Luck. This can be achieved by upgrading your luck stat in the main menu or by equipping slimes like the Golden Spike.

**Q: Is it worth it to rebirth as soon as I hit the requirement?**

A: Generally, yes. The permanent luck multiplier gained from a **slime rebirth** is much more valuable than the gold you lose. The only exception is if you are very close to rolling a high-tier slime and want to use your current gold for a few more rolls.
