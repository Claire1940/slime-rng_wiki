## Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you find that the **Slime RNG level up slimes** trick isn't working for you, check the following variables:

1.  **Party Size:** Having a full party of four is recommended, but some players suggest that party size can slightly alter spawn timings.

3.  **Menu Speed:** The "Treasure Map" trick relies on the game not having enough time to generate a standard spawn. If you linger in the menu for more than 2-3 seconds, the RNG will advance, potentially ruining the setup.
