Luck is the most important stat in any RNG-based game. By investing your **Slime RNG coins** into permanent luck upgrades, you shift the odds in your favor. Even a 5% increase can be the difference between a week of grinding and a lucky 10-minute session.

The "Roll Speed" upgrade allows you to process more slimes per minute. Since each roll is a potential source of income and progress, this should be upgraded alongside luck. In the 2026 version of the game, maxing out your roll speed can lead to a roll every 0.5 seconds.

## Using Potions and Auras to Boost Income

Potions are temporary consumables that can drastically alter your earning potential. In the Slime RNG shop, you will find various brews that last anywhere from 10 minutes to an hour.

*   **Fortune Brew**: Increases the value of slimes sold for a short duration. Use this when you have a backlog of rare slimes to sell.
*   **Haste Elixir**: Temporarily maximizes roll speed regardless of your current permanent upgrades.
*   **Golden Touch Potion**: Converts a percentage of common rolls into "Golden" rolls, which yield significantly more **Slime RNG coins**.

> **Warning:** Do not stack multiple potions of the same type unless the UI explicitly states they are additive. Often, the timer just refreshes rather than doubling the effect.
