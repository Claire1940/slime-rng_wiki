## Combat and World Progression

Slime RNG isn't just about the luck of the draw; it’s about how you use those slimes in the field. Sending your team to battle enemy slimes is the most direct way to earn the coins needed for high-level upgrades. Each new world you unlock features tougher enemies but significantly higher rewards.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Don't rush into a new world if your slimes are under-leveled. You will earn XP and coins much slower if it takes you a long time to defeat a single enemy. It is often better to "over-farm" an easier area until you can dominate the next zone.

### Team Composition Tips

When selecting which slimes to equip, look at their individual stats rather than just their rarity. A well-balanced team usually consists of:
1.  **The Tank:** A slime with high health to soak up damage.
2.  **The DPS:** Your rarest, highest-damage slimes.
3.  **The Support:** Slimes that provide luck or coin buffs while equipped.

As mentioned earlier in this **Slime RNG XP guide**, expanding your slime slots should be a priority. A squad of five decent slimes will almost always outperform a squad of two amazing slimes due to the sheer volume of attacks and utility they provide.
