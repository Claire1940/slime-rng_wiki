1.  **Open the Settings Menu:** Look for the gear icon on the left side of your screen.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Slime RNG auto roll feature free for all players?**

A: Yes, the basic auto roll feature is free and can be found in the game settings. However, some advanced "Fast Roll" upgrades or "Auto-Potion" features may require in-game currency or gamepasses to unlock.

**Q: Can I get banned for using a Slime RNG auto roll script?**

A: While the developers of Slime RNG are generally lenient toward automation (as it is a core part of the RNG genre), using third-party scripts always carries a minor risk. Most players use them without issue, but it is always safer to stick to the built-in game features if you are worried about your account.

**Q: Why does my auto roll keep stopping?**

A: The most common reason is a full inventory. If your "Auto-Delete" filters are not set high enough, your storage will fill up with common slimes, which prevents the game from rolling new ones. Check your settings and ensure you are deleting anything below a certain rarity.

**Q: Does luck affect the speed of the Slime RNG auto roll?**

A: No, luck only affects the rarity of the slimes you find. To increase the speed of your rolls, you need to equip specific items that provide "Roll Speed" or "Cooldown Reduction" stats. These are usually obtained through crafting or by defeating world bosses.
