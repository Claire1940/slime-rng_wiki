## Understanding Base Luck and Multipliers

Before diving into complex potion recipes, you must understand how luck is calculated. Your "Base Luck" is influenced by your permanent upgrades and equipped slimes. Every roll you take is a product of this base luck multiplied by any active temporary boosts.

For example, if your base luck is 1.5x and you use a 2x Potion, your total luck isn't 3.5x—it scales based on the game's internal RNG weight. In 2026, the meta focuses on "multiplicative stacking" rather than additive stacking. This means that using a high-tier luck potion during a specific biome event is far more effective than using ten low-tier potions in a neutral environment.

Heavenly potions provide a more consistent return on investment for the average player. Bound potions are often considered a "sink" for late-game players who have an excess of materials. 1.  **Farm Lucky Potions:** These are the base for almost every major craft.

3.  **Avoid the Jester Scam:** NPCs like the Jester or "Official" often sell items at a massive markup. Only buy from them if you have millions of spare coins and are missing a single ingredient for a major craft.
