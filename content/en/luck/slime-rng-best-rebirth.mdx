Rebirth gives you more luck, and luck improves long-term slime quality. That part is obvious. What many players miss is that **rebirth timing** determines whether luck gains feel powerful or pointless.

- You delay your next luck bump
- You waste active grind time on low-value income

If you rebirth too early:
- You may not have enough base economy to recover smoothly
- Your next cycle can stall if damage and income are too low

The best approach is to rebirth at the point where your next upgrade costs start rising faster than your farming speed. That’s the practical sweet spot for a **slime rng best rebirth** route.
