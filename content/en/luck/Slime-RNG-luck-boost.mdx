## FAQ

**Q: Does the Slime RNG luck boost affect all items in the game?**

A: Yes, the luck multipliers in Sailor Piece, including those from the Slime Island skill tree, apply to all mob and boss drops, including materials, weapons, and accessories.

**Q: Can I stack multiple luck specs on different weapons?**

A: No, the luck bonus is only active for the item you are currently holding. However, you can apply a spec to your fruit and another to your sword, and the game will calculate the luck based on whichever one is currently equipped in your hand.

**Q: Is the Kitsune race better than Warlord for farming?**

A: Absolutely. While Warlord is excellent for PvP and raw damage, Kitsune provides a 25% luck boost that is essential for increasing drop rates. If your goal is farming, Kitsune is the superior choice.

**Q: How do I get the Radiant Rune for my luck build?**

A: The Radiant Rune is a rare drop obtained by completing floors in the Towers. It provides a 28% luck boost, making it one of the strongest runes in the game for farmers.
