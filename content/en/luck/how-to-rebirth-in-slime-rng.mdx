## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to learn how to rebirth in slime rng as a beginner?**

A: Start with short rebirth cycles and a strict upgrade order: income first, then farming pace, then roll speed. Keep your rebuild simple and repeatable before experimenting with longer pushes.

**Q: Do I lose all progress when I rebirth in Slime RNG?**

A: You generally lose run-based economy and progression upgrades, but your core slime collection remains. That’s why strong slimes and XP transfer strategy matter so much for fast recovery.

**Q: Should I chase rare slimes before my first few rebirths?**

A: Usually, no. Early progression is smoother when you prioritize consistent farming and rebirth frequency. Rare-chasing becomes more efficient once your luck scaling and rebuild speed are stronger.

**Q: How often should I rebirth if I’m stuck mid-game?**

A: Rebirth when upgrade costs outpace your farming growth and your cycle efficiency drops. Track cycle time; if each run takes longer without meaningful gains, reset earlier and tighten your rebuild order.
