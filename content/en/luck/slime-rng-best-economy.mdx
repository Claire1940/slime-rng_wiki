## Plort Market Strategy: Sell Cycles, Not Panic Drops

The Plort Market responds to your sales behavior. If you dump one plort nonstop, value can dip. If you hold inventory and rotate sales, you can catch stronger prices.

| Market Action | Short-Term Result | Best Use Case | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Daily bulk sell** | Fast cash now | Early upgrade rush | Lower average value over time |
| **Staggered rotation** | Better average price | Mid-game stable ranch | Requires storage discipline |
| **Spike dumping** | Maximum burst profit | High silo capacity | Missed timing if you wait too long |

Use this 4-day cycle:

1. Feed and collect normally  
2. Store most plorts in silo  
3. Watch market board each morning  
4. Sell heavily only when your target plort rises above your baseline  

> **💡 Tip:** Keep at least two profitable plort lines active. If one price dips, the other can carry your cash flow.

### Recovery Protocol After a Ranch Crash

If a Tarr event wipes a lane:

- Vacuum all threats first.
- Re-seed gardens before re-adding slimes.
- Rebuild with one stable Largo type per pen.
- Restore market rhythm in 2–3 days.

This recovery method keeps your **slime rng best economy** run alive even after mistakes.
