## Advanced Farming and AFK Strategies

To truly maximize the impact of your **Slime RNG luck**, you must adopt an AFK (Away From Keyboard) mindset. Most successful players in 2026 utilize the auto-roll feature in conjunction with specific filters. Most RNG games allow you to set a "rarity filter," which automatically deletes common slimes and only keeps the ones that meet your criteria.

This prevents your inventory from filling up with "trash" slimes while you are sleeping or at work. By setting your filter to "Epic" or higher, you ensure that every slot in your inventory is occupied by a slime that actually improves your squad's power.
