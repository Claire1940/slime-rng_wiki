## Advanced Mechanics: Big Dice and Crafting

The late-game meta for **Slime RNG rare slimes** introduces specialized items that bypass standard RNG. If you are struggling to pull a specific rarity, you should look into the Crafting Machine and specialized consumables.

### The Power of Big Dice
Big Dice are rare items often obtained through the Index or special events. When used, they guarantee that your next roll will result in a "Big" variant of a slime. While the specific slime species is still RNG-dependent, the "Big" modifier significantly boosts the health and damage of the result.

### Crafting Recipes
Located in the Heaven area, the Crafting Machine allows you to combine lower-tier materials and slimes into powerful artifacts. Recipes are hidden throughout the world:
