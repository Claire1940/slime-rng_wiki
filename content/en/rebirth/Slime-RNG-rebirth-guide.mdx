## Understanding the Rebirth System

In Slime RNG, a rebirth is a strategic reset of your current progress. When you choose to rebirth, you lose your current slime inventory and your accumulated coins. In exchange, you receive "Rebirth Points" or permanent luck multipliers that make your future rolls significantly more successful. This mechanic is designed to prevent the game from becoming stagnant and to give players a reason to keep engaging with the core rolling loop.

The primary benefit of rebirthing is the exponential increase in luck. In the early stages of the game, finding a "Mythic" or "Legendary" slime can take thousands of rolls. However, after several rebirths, your base luck increases to the point where these rare slimes become common occurrences. This allows you to hunt for the truly elite "Cosmic" or "Divine" slimes that are otherwise nearly impossible to obtain.

> **Warning:** Before initiating a rebirth, ensure you have spent all your remaining coins on permanent upgrades that persist through resets. Rebirthing with a full wallet of unspent coins is a common mistake that slows down your overall progression.

## FAQ

**Q: Does rebirthing remove my equipped auras?**

A: Yes, rebirthing resets your current slime inventory, which includes your equipped auras. However, any permanent skins or gamepasses you have purchased will remain active. Following a **Slime RNG rebirth guide** helps you prepare for this reset so you can quickly re-equip high-tier slimes.

**Q: When is the best time to rebirth for the first time?**

A: You should rebirth as soon as you hit the 100,000 coin requirement and have at least 10 unique slimes. Delaying your first rebirth is inefficient because the 25% luck boost you receive will make earning that next 100,000 coins much faster.

**Q: Can I keep my "Shiny" slimes after a rebirth?**

A: No, all slimes are removed upon rebirth. The only way to keep slimes is to use a "Slime Cage" item, which is a rare drop that allows you to save one slime through a single rebirth cycle.

**Q: How do I increase my roll speed permanently?**

A: Roll speed is primarily increased through specific rebirth milestones (like Rebirth 5) and by purchasing "Speed Upgrades" in the main shop using coins. Some rare artifacts found in later stages of the game also provide permanent speed reductions to the roll interval.
