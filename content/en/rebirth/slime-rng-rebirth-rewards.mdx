## Essential Preparation Steps

Successfully executing a rebirth requires more than just holding the required amount of currency. You must optimize your loadout and ensure your active equipment is aligned with your goals. Many players make the mistake of keeping inefficient items equipped during the transition.

## Common Rebirth Mistakes to Avoid

Even experienced players occasionally trip up when managing their resets. Avoiding these common pitfalls will save you hours of redundant grinding.

*   **Rebirthing too early:** Don't rush to the minimum requirement if you are close to a major upgrade that could boost your passive income.
*   **Ignoring Luck Upgrades:** Some players prioritize coins over luck. In the long run, luck is the primary gatekeeper for high-tier content.
*   **Unequipping Buffs:** Ensure your active equipment bonuses are active during the transition, as some items provide passive buffs that persist through the reset process.

## FAQ

**Q: What happens to my slimes when I use the rebirth system?**

A: In most cases, your collection remains, but your currency and specific progress stats are reset to provide you with the permanent multipliers associated with the **slime rng rebirth rewards**.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many times I can rebirth?**

A: Currently, there is no hard cap on the number of rebirths, though the requirements increase significantly with each tier to balance the game's economy.

**Q: Does the luck multiplier stack with other items?**

A: Yes, the rebirth multiplier is multiplicative, meaning it scales your base luck and any other temporary buffs you may have active, making it the most powerful tool in your arsenal.

**Q: How do I know if I am ready for the next rebirth?**

A: You are generally ready when you have reached the coin threshold and have hit a wall where your current luck stat is no longer yielding significant progress on your collection.
