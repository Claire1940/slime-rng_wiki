## Slime RNG rebirth cost explained (and why it matters)

The rebirth system is a trade: you give up current money to gain stronger long-term luck growth. That means the **Slime RNG rebirth cost** is not just an entry fee—it is a timing check. If you rebirth when your current coin income has slowed, your next run becomes faster because your luck multiplier improves your roll quality and index progression.

Think about rebirth in loops:

1. Build coin income and roll speed.
2. Spend all coins on permanent-value upgrades.
3. Rebirth when upgrade prices become inefficient.
4. Use new luck to rebuild faster and unlock better slimes.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Don’t sit on large unspent coins before resetting. Convert coins into upgrades first, because money is what you lose on rebirth.

### Core rebirth logic in simple terms

| Rebirth factor | Short-term effect | Long-term effect | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Money reset** | Slower for a few minutes | Forces efficient spending loops | **Medium** |
| **Luck increase** | Better roll odds soon after reset | Strong compounding over many runs | **High** |
| **Index progression** | Better rewards over time | Permanent luck/coin support | **High** |
| **Multiple rebirths** | Bigger immediate boost if affordable | Speeds mid-game progression | **High** |

When players search for **Slime RNG rebirth cost**, they usually want a fixed number. In reality, update balance can shift thresholds. Your best move is to optimize around efficiency breakpoints, not a single static price target.

## What you keep vs what you lose after rebirth

A lot of confusion comes from not knowing what resets. Here is the clean framework you should use every time.

| Category | After rebirth | What to do before pressing rebirth |
|---|---|---|
| **Coins / currency** | **Lost** | Spend everything on upgrades first |
| **Slimes** | **Kept** | Equip best team before farming next run |
| **Upgrades** | Generally **kept** (progression upgrades) | Prioritize value upgrades before reset |
| **Items / boosts** | **Kept** if inventory-based | Activate only when actively farming |
| **Luck from rebirth** | **Increases** | Stack with friend luck and index bonuses |

This is why the **Slime RNG rebirth cost** is worth paying repeatedly. You are sacrificing temporary coins to gain scaling power that improves all future sessions.

> **💡 Tip:** If a run feels “stuck” where upgrades are expensive and coin gain is flattening, that is often your signal to rebirth.
