## Candy and Crystal Area Recipes

As you move into the more advanced biomes, the **Slime RNG recipe locations** become slightly more difficult to spot. These areas are designed to test your observational skills and your ability to navigate the terrain.

### The Candy Area
The Candy area is filled with giant sweets and chocolate obstacles. To find the recipe here, look for the massive chocolate bar structure. This isn't just decoration; it serves as a landmark for a secret. If you walk directly behind the chocolate bar, you will find the recipe tucked into the crevice between the candy and the boundary wall.

### The Crystal Area
The Crystal area contains perhaps the most challenging recipe to acquire. This biome is dominated by a massive mountain that towers over the rest of the zone. You cannot find this recipe on the ground level. Instead, you must climb to the very peak of the mountain.
